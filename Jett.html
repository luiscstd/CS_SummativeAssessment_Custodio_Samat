Who is Jett?

Coming form South Korea, Jett is an agent classified as a Duelist. On Attacking, a duelist is an agent who is given utilities that allows them to enter site and clear it of enemies in order to let their teammates plant the Spike and win the round. On defending, a duelist has the utilities to take on aggressive and risky plays and is expected to do so since it is a part of its role.

Abilities

Like all agents, Jett is given four abilities: Updraft, Tailwind, Cloudburst, and Blade Storm.

First Ability: Updraft

Jett's updraft allows her to dash upwards. This allows her to get into positions, get information, and to outplay the enemy team.

Second Ability: Tailwind

Tailwind is an ability that allows Jett to immediately dash into differnt directions, and this allows her to reposition fast to get away from danger or to outplay the enemy.

Third Ability: Cloudburst

Cloudburst is an ability that allows Jett to smoke certain areas. These are smokes that she can cast and control, but they only last for a short amount of time.

Ultimate: Blade Storm

Jett's Blade Storm is her Ultimte ability. When activated, Jett is given a set of deadly and accurate knives that return when she is able to get a kill.
