Raze

Who is Raze?

All the way from Brazil, Raze is an agent classified as a Duelist. On Attacking, a duelist is an agent who is given utilities that allows them to enter site and clear it of enemies in order to let their teammates plant the Spike and win the round. On defending, a duelist has the utilities to take on aggressive and risky plays and is expected to do so since it is a part of its role.

Abilities

Like all agents, Raze is given four abilities: Blast Pack, Paint Shells, Boom Bot, and Showstopper

First Ability: Blast Pack

Raze is given Blast Packs that can damage the enemy and boost her to get into positions in an instant.

Second Ability: Paint Shells

Raze's Paint Shells act as a grenade that explode with such force and such a short amount of time. This helps Raze get easy kills in an instant.

Third Ability: Boom Bot

Her Boom Bot is small moving bot that scans an area for enemies, and if an enemy is found, it chases the enemy then explodes to damage or kill the enemy.

Ultimate Ability: Showstopper

Raze's Showstopper is her Ultimate Ability. The Showstopper is a powerful rocket launcher with such explosive force that it can wipe teams; however, there is a timer, so shoot fast!
